Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Trucker, an app designed to help truck drivers manage trips, fuel receipts, and earnings. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using Trucker, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Username, email address, driver name, company name, and truck number provided during sign-up.
  • Trip Data: Trip details such as trip number, origin, destination, date, load price, and distance.
  • Fuel Data: Fuel receipt details including city, state/province, quantity, price, and optional images.
  • Device Information: Limited device data (e.g., for crash reporting) may be collected if analytics are enabled (with your consent).

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide core app functionality, such as saving trips, calculating earnings, and managing fuel receipts.
  • Send one-time passwords (OTPs) via email for password reset requests.
  • Improve the app through optional analytics (with your consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

How We Store and Protect Your Information

  • Storage: Personal information is stored locally on your device using Core Data. Passwords are securely stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures, such as Keychain for passwords and temporary OTP storage with expiration.
  • Data Retention: Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete your account to remove all data (contact support for assistance).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • SwiftSMTP: To send OTP emails for password resets. Your email address is shared with our SMTP provider (Google) solely for this purpose.
  • No other third-party services collect or process your data unless explicitly stated and consented to.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • To comply with legal requirements or respond to lawful requests.
  • With your consent, for optional analytics or other services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information by managing your account or contacting support.
  • Opt out of optional data collection (e.g., analytics) via app settings.
  • Request information about how your data is used.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: support@truckerapp.com
  • Address: Trucker App Support, 123 Main St, Toronto, ON, Canada

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
